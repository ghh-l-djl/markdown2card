## Design Context

### Users
Obsidian users who turn Markdown notes into polished social-media cards and need fast, dependable authoring and export controls on desktop.

### Brand Personality
Independent, warm, inventive. The interface should feel like a carefully built creative tool rather than a generic SaaS dashboard.

### Aesthetic Direction
An organic hazelnut-workshop identity combining warm cream and brown surfaces with restrained technical accents. Support both Obsidian light and dark themes, keep content readable, and avoid generic neon AI visuals.

### Design Principles
- Keep the note-to-card workflow fast and visually calm.
- Use strong hierarchy and asymmetric editorial composition.
- Preserve high contrast across arbitrary Obsidian themes.
- Make important actions unmistakable and keyboard accessible.
- Adapt layouts at content-driven breakpoints instead of merely shrinking them.
